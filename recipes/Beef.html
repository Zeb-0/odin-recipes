Beef Stew

Description

This hearty Kenyan-style beef stew is made with tender beef simmered in a rich tomato and onion base. It's simple, flavorful, and perfect with ugali, rice, or chapati.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (cubed)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger (optional)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 beef cube or 1 tsp Royco (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper (optional)
  • 1 to 1½ cups water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Add onions and sauté until soft.
  2. Add garlic and ginger (if using). Stir briefly.
  3. Add beef cubes. Brown the meat for 5 - 7 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes. Cook until soft and saucy.
  5. Mix in beef cube or Royco (optional).
  6. Add water and bring to a boil.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat for 30 - 45 mins until tender.
  8. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
