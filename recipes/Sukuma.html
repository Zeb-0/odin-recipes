Sukuma

Description

Sukuma Wiki is a popular East African dish made primarily with collard greens (sukuma wiki means "push the week" in Swahili, reflecting its role as an affordable staple food).

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (collard greens), finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or cooking oil of your choice)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 beef cube or Royco (optional, for flavor)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop sukuma wiki.
  2. Heat oil, fry onions until soft.
  3. Add garlic (optional), cook briefly.
  4. Add tomatoes, cook until soft.
  5. Mix in beef cube or Royco (optional).
  6. Add sukuma wiki, stir and cook for 5 - 7 mins.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
