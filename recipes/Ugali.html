Ugali

Description

Ugali is the most common main East African dish particularly in Kenya and Tanzania. It is also found in sevral other parts of Africa under different names such as 'sadza' in Zimbabwe, 'nsima' in Malawi and 'fufu' in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • 1litre Water

Steps

  • bring the water to a boil under medium to high heat and add some flour
  • Stir to form a thick porridge
  • add small amounts of the flour as your mix to a thick mash
  • covr the pot and allow to cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • your Ugali is ready to serve
